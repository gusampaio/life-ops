# Principles

A ledger of decisions made from lived experience.

**Rule:** only add a principle after you've actually lived it — not from reading, not aspirationally. If you haven't paid the cost of learning it, it doesn't belong here yet.

Format: `### N. Title` + explanation of what you learned and why it matters + the date you added it.
